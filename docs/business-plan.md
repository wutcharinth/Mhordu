# หมอดู.ai — Business Plan

**Brand:** หมอดู.ai (Thai) · Mhordu (Latin transliteration)
**Domain:** mhordu.ai
**Founder:** Solo, bootstrap (THB 0–500k)
**Scope:** Thailand only — no SEA expansion. Depth over breadth.
**Date:** 2026-05-13

---

## 1. Context

Thai consumers spend heavily on fortune-telling — โหราศาสตร์ไทย, ดวงชะตา, ฤกษ์ดี, สีมงคล are embedded in daily decisions (weddings, business launches, baby names, lottery, home moves). The market is fragmented across (a) in-person หมอดู (THB 500–5,000 per session, repeat 2–12×/year), (b) Facebook pages of celebrity astrologers, (c) generic horoscope apps with shallow Western astrology, and (d) sketchy LINE bots with no cultural depth.

Two structural openings:
1. **LINE is where Thais live.** ~56M LINE users in Thailand, daily ritual usage. A LINE-native fortune-teller meets users in their existing habit, no app install friction.
2. **AI is finally good enough — but only with curated Thai cultural context.** Generic LLMs butcher โหราศาสตร์ไทย (they default to Western zodiac, miss ฤกษ์, mistranslate cultural concepts). A purpose-built knowledge base + Thai-tuned prompts is a defensible moat.

Mhordu is the LINE-native AI fortune-teller for Thai cultural astrology, monetizing via subscriptions and viral two-player features (ดวงคู่ / love compatibility).

## 3. Problem

**For Thai consumers:**
- In-person หมอดู: expensive, hard to schedule, quality varies, no follow-up
- Facebook celebrity astrologers: broadcast not personal, paid DMs are slow, can't verify
- Generic horoscope apps (Co–Star, Sanook ดวง): shallow, Western, mistranslate Thai concepts
- LINE bots that exist: low quality, no real AI, scripted templates

**Underserved jobs-to-be-done:**
1. *Daily ritual* — quick check before work, paired with morning coffee
2. *Decision support* — "should I take this job?", "ฤกษ์ดี for moving house?", "is this person compatible?"
3. *Lottery / timing* — anticipated by Thais even when entertainment-framed
4. *Anxiety reassurance* — "will it be okay?" emotional support, esp. relationships and money
5. *Social signaling* — sharing readings with friends, comparing compatibility

## 5. Market Analysis

### TAM / SAM / SOM (Thailand only)

| Layer | Definition | Size |
|---|---|---|
| **TAM** | Thai adults who consume fortune content monthly | ~25–30M people |
| **SAM** | LINE-active Thai adults age 18–55 willing to pay digitally | ~15–18M people |
| **SOM (3-year)** | Realistic capture given GTM | 500k–1M MAU, 30–60k paid |

**Validation signals:**
- Thai fortune-telling market estimated THB 8–15B/year across in-person, products, media
- LINE penetration: ~70% of Thai population, ~95% of smartphone users
- Thai mobile payment penetration: ~85% (TrueMoney, LINE Pay, PromptPay)
- Cultural willingness to pay: Thais routinely pay THB 200–2,000 per reading, recurring

### Why Thailand-only is the right call
- **Cultural moat is non-transferable.** โหราศาสตร์ไทย is specific to Thailand — Vietnamese tử vi, Indonesian primbon, Chinese ba zi are entirely separate systems that need their own knowledge bases. Expansion = building three new products, not scaling one.
- **THB 8–15B Thai market is enough.** Capturing even 1% of the recurring spend is THB 80–150M annual revenue — well past sustainable scale for a small team.
- **Depth wins repeat.** Doubling down on Thai cultural authority (monk advisory, real หมอดู partnerships, regional dialect tuning) creates defensibility that horizontal expansion cannot.
- **Solo founder bandwidth.** Each new market doubles ops cost: payments, language QA, regulatory, content licensing, marketing. Thailand-deep keeps the founder shippable.

### Thailand-deep growth lanes (instead of SEA)
- **B2B ฤกษ์ services** — Thai SMBs (shop opens, weddings, signings) is a high-ARPU vertical with low CAC
- **White-label หมอดู platform** — real Thai astrologers run their practice on Mhordu's tech, take a revenue share
- **Annual seasonal campaigns** — Songkran, Loy Krathong, Thai New Year ฤกษ์ + lucky reads
- **Sticker pack / LINE theme** — Thai LINE sticker market is enormous; Mhordu IP makes sense
- **Physical event series** — annual "ดวงปีหน้า" reading nights in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket
- **Premium tier expansion** — coverage of more Thai astrology traditions (เลข ๗ ตัว, ลายมือ Thai, นรลักษณ์, สมุนไพร)

## 7. Cultural Moat — The Knowledge Base Strategy

This is the company's core IP. Build it deliberately.

**Phase 1 (months 1–2):** Founder-curated knowledge base from canonical Thai sources:
- โหราศาสตร์ไทยเดิม (Thai traditional astrology) — foundational
- มหาทักษา (8 directions / planetary day system)
- ฤกษ์งามยามดี (auspicious timing) — calendar logic
- สีมงคลประจำวัน (lucky colors by day of birth)
- ดาวพระเคราะห์ (planetary placements, Thai system)
- ดวงคู่แต่งงาน (marriage compatibility traditions)
- คัมภีร์เลข ๗ ตัว (Lek 7 Tua numerology)

Output: 200–500 page structured knowledge base (markdown + JSON), embedded into vector DB.

**Phase 2 (months 3–6):** Add expert advisory:
- 1–2 retainer relationships with mid-tier Thai astrologers (THB 5–15k/month each)
- Quarterly review of AI-generated readings for cultural accuracy
- Public credit / collab posts as marketing

**Phase 3 (year 2):** Monk / temple advisory:
- Buddhist sensitivity review board
- Donate % of revenue to temple as goodwill (also marketing)
- Avoid contradicting Buddhist teachings

**Quality gates:**
- Native Thai reviewer on every prompt template before ship
- Monthly accuracy audit (sample 100 readings, score 1–5)
- User feedback loop: 👍/👎 on every reading, retrain prompts monthly

## 10. Tech Stack & Operations

Lean, pay-per-use, AI-leveraged. Solo founder must keep ops cost <10% of revenue at scale.

| Layer | Choice | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Chat surface** | LINE Messaging API | Where users are |
| **Web** | Next.js on Vercel | Fast, free tier covers early traffic |
| **Backend** | Supabase (Postgres + auth) or AWS Lambda + DynamoDB | Pay-per-use, no devops |
| **Vector DB** | pgvector in Supabase | Co-located with data, cheap |
| **LLM** | Claude Sonnet 4.6 (reasoning), Haiku 4.5 (cheap reads) | Best Thai language quality |
| **Image gen** | fal.ai or Replicate (Stable Diffusion XL + LoRA) | Thai-styled fortune cards |
| **Voice (later)** | Botnoi or VAJA Thai TTS | Native Thai voice |
| **Payment** | Omise (Thai gateway) | Single integration: LINE Pay, TrueMoney, PromptPay, cards |
| **Analytics** | PostHog free tier | Self-host later if scale demands |
| **Email/SMS fallback** | Resend + Twilio | If user disables LINE |

### Cost structure at scale
- LLM inference: THB 5–30 per active user/month at scale (negotiable with volume)
- LINE Messaging API: variable per push; pull / reply free
- Image generation: THB 0.10–2.00 per card
- Infrastructure: THB 10–30k/month at 100k MAU
- **Target gross margin at 50k MAU: 65–75%**

## 13. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Thai Lottery Act §17** (predicting lottery numbers illegal) | High | High | Hard ban on lottery numbers. Frame all readings as entertainment. Disclaimer in TOS. |

| **Buddhist / monk community pushback** | Low | High | Respectful framing. Donate % to temple. Advisory board. Avoid contradicting dharma. |
| **Payment / fraud / chargebacks** | Medium | Medium | Omise handles fraud detection. Subscription-only model limits one-shot fraud. |
| **Data privacy (Thai PDPA)** | Medium | High | Compliant data handling from day 1. Thai lawyer review before launch. Minimal data collection. |
| **LLM cost spikes** | Medium | Medium | Multi-provider (Claude + open-source fallback). Caching. Aggressive prompt-shortening at scale. |
| **Competitor copies core feature** | High | Low | Cultural moat takes 12+ months to replicate. Brand trust harder still. |

### 16.2 Visual Direction — DECIDED: Mix A + B (dual register)

**Locked decision:** dual register. Modern Thai Mystical (A) for premium / web / B2B surfaces. Y2K Mystic Pop (B) for daily share card / viral content / IG Stories. Single shared logo bridges both. Voice: **พี่** (friendly older sister) — softens A's seriousness and pairs natively with B.

**Register split (per impeccable's `register` field):**
- **Brand register (A — Modern Thai Mystical)**: mhordu.ai landing, pricing, cultural authority page, paywall, ฤกษ์ business/B2B surfaces, premium-tier UI
- **Product register (B — Y2K Mystic Pop)**: daily horoscope card, ดวงคู่ compatibility card, lucky color tile, sticker pack, all share-optimized artifacts

**Shared system:**
- One logo, used on both (lotus + planet glyph monoline)
- One Thai display serif (Bai Jamjuree or Pridi) — anchors A, used as accent in B's bouncy world
- พี่ voice across both — A is พี่ being thoughtful, B is พี่ being playful

**Three directions retained below for reference / future pivots.**

#### Direction A · Modern Thai Mystical (Eastern Philosophy school)
- **Scene sentence:** A 32-year-old Thai woman reads her daily fortune on the BTS at 8am, single glance, then forwards to her mom on LINE.
- **Reference DNA:** Kenya Hara / Muji minimalism × Thai temple frescoes × หนังสือพระคัมภีร์ paper texture
- **Palette (Committed):** cream paper `oklch(95% 0.02 80)`, deep indigo ink `oklch(22% 0.06 270)`, single gold accent `oklch(72% 0.13 80)` ≤10%
- **Type:** Thai display serif (Bai Jamjuree / Pridi / Trirong) + LINE Seed Sans TH body
- **Mark:** abstract lotus + planet glyph, monoline
- **Mood:** contemplative, premium, respectful — *like a leather-bound astrology book in modern format*
- **Best for:** adult / professional audience, ฤกษ์ wedding & business users, premium tier credibility
- **Risk:** less viral / less Gen Z

#### Direction B · Y2K Mystic Pop (Experimental Pioneer school)
- **Scene sentence:** A 19-year-old Thai uni student screenshots her daily fortune, drops a cute emoji caption, posts to IG Story before lunch.
- **Reference DNA:** late-90s Thai pop magazines × Sailor Moon × Y2K revival × Tamagotchi
- **Palette (Full palette):** pastel butter `oklch(92% 0.10 95)`, lavender `oklch(82% 0.08 290)`, holographic gold gradient (allowed as brand signature), soft black ink
- **Type:** bouncy display Thai (custom or licensed kawaii Thai serif) + LINE Seed Sans TH
- **Mark:** mhordu-chan mascot — small celestial creature, multiple expression states for rotating daily uses

### 16.4 Thai-Specific Design Rules

| Concern | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Line-height** | ≥1.8 for Thai body text — vowel marks above/below need room. Latin-default 1.5 will break Thai. |
| **No word boundaries** | Thai script runs without spaces. Use color, weight, line breaks as chunking. Don't rely on tracking. |
| **สีมงคล collision** | Don't pick a brand color that conflicts with the seven day-of-birth lucky colors users will read in-app. Cream / off-white / black-as-ink are safe; saturated red / yellow / green can clash. |
| **Numerals** | Use Thai numerals (๐–๙) for spiritual / mystical content, Arabic (0–9) for prices / dates / data. Mixing them ad-hoc looks amateur. |
| **Religious imagery** | Lotus, planet glyphs, ฤกษ์ wheels, mythical creatures (นาค, ครุฑ) are fair game. Buddha imagery, monk imagery, specific deity depictions require monk advisory clearance — defer to year 2. |
| **Thai font licensing** | LINE Seed Sans TH is free / open. Pridi and Trirong via Google Fonts (Cadson Demak) are free. Commercial Thai display fonts (Sukhumvit Set, FC Subject) need licensing — budget THB 5–30k for the right display face. |
| **Emoji compatibility** | Default Apple / Google emoji renders inconsistently. For brand consistency, ship custom inline SVG glyphs in cards, not unicode emoji. |
| **Daily card composition** | Plan for 35–55 Thai characters per headline maximum (Thai is denser than Latin). Test at LINE preview thumbnail size (180×180). |

### 16.5 Design Workflow (impeccable → huashu)

1. **Teach** — write `PRODUCT.md` (Mhordu brand, audience, anti-references). Pass impeccable's preflight.
2. **Shape** — translate the chosen direction into a one-page design brief: scene, color strategy, type stack, layout grammar, motion principles. User confirms before any HTML.
3. **Probe / mood** — `nano-banana-pro` or equivalent: AI-generate 6–9 visual references (mood board) in the chosen direction. Not final designs — alignment check.
4. **Craft (hero first)** — build the daily horoscope card. 3 variations on shared system. Iterate to user-confirmed "right."
5. **Critique** — 5-dimension review (philosophy / hierarchy / detail / function / innovation). Score ≥ 8/10 before extending.
6. **Document** — extract `DESIGN.md` tokens once one screen is locked.
7. **Extend** — apply the locked system to remaining screens (LINE flow, web). Re-critique batch.
8. **Harden** — accessibility (Thai screen-reader support), responsive sizing, dark-mode if applicable, edge cases (long names, missing birth time).
9. **Launch** — ship hero card + onboarding flow to closed beta (200 users), measure share rate.

### 16.6 Anti-References (what Mhordu will NOT look like)

- ❌ Generic mystical-purple gradient + neon stars (Co-Star direction — saturated category default)
- ❌ Sketchy Thai Facebook page banner (gradient text + Comic-Sans-Thai)
- ❌ Stock astrology iconography (Western zodiac glyphs) — Mhordu is Thai cultural, not Western
- ❌ Casino / lottery aesthetic (gold-glitter, slot-machine framing) — legal + brand risk
- ❌ ChatGPT-default Material-UI card grid look
- ❌ Generic SaaS "hero metric template" on the web landing
- ❌ Side-stripe colored borders, glassmorphism, gradient text — impeccable's absolute bans
